Procedure:-
- Open **Microsoft Excel**.
- Enter your data in a table format with columns like **Product**, **Quantity Sold**, and **Price per Unit**.
- Click on the first empty cell in a new column (e.g., **D2**) and type: `=B2*C2` to calculate sales for the first product.
- Press **Enter**.
- Click the fill handle (small square at the bottom-right of the cell) and drag it down to calculate sales for all 5 products.
- Click an empty cell below the sales column (e.g., **D7**) to calculate total sales.
- Type: `=SUM(D2:D6)` and press **Enter**.
- The cell now shows the **total sales amount** for all 5 products.
