Procedure:-
1. Click the **Start** menu.
2. Search for **Disk Cleanup**.
3. Open the **Disk Cleanup** tool.
4. Select the drive you want to clean (usually **C:**).
5. Click **OK**.
6. Check the boxes for the files you want to delete (like temporary files, recycle bin, etc.).
7. Click **OK**, then click **Delete Files** to confirm.
Disk cleanup is performed.
