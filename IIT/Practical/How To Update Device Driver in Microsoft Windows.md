Procedure:-
1. Click the **Start** menu.
2. Search for **Device Manager** and open it.
3. Find the device you want to update (e.g., Display adapters, Sound, etc.).
4. Right-click the device.
5. Click **Update driver**.
6. Choose one option:
    - **Search automatically for drivers** (Windows finds it for you)
    - **Browse my computer for drivers** (if you already have the driver file)
7. Follow the instructions to complete the update.
Device drivers are updated in Microsoft windows.
